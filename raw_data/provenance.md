
## Qualtrics exports 
All files have a date indicated their download time. All are SPSS format.
- vacc_se_prescreen_fullXXX.csv: Qualtrics export of prescreening survey
- flu_survey_mainXXX.sav: Qualtrics export of main survey/experiment module
- flu_vacc_se_followupXXX.sav: Qualtrics export of follow up survey


## Prolific demogprahics
Exported demographic information from prolifics plaform, for participants signing up for 
 each  qualtrics survey, including people who did not start or abandoned the survey 
- prolific_demographic_export_692494f77a877e57e000eb60.csv: Prescreen demographics
- prolific_demographic_export_main.csv: Main survey participants who signed up at base pay
- prolific_demographic_export_main_morepay.csv: Main survey participants who signed up 
    at higher pay posting 
- prolific_demographic_export_followup.csv: Followup participants
